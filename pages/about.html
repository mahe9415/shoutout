About

In a day to day life a common man faces lot of civil and social issues like no proper water system, no proper roads, light problem etc. As a common man when he raising his voice about this issues it didn't reaches the respective officers/government.

Like everyone knows unity is strength. Instead of raising voice alone we thought if we shout as a group of people the respective/government would hear the voice and leads to take action for that issue

A common man can login into this application by using his FB id. He can create a Campaign for his problem. Let say if he has a problem of no proper road facility in his street. He can create campaign as Road problem in Madurai Streets. This campaign will be posted in the Shoutout.org page in the FB and that post will be send to his friends in the FB messenger. When the Campaigners friends clicked the post that will take them to the

Shoutout.org application. He/She should login with his FB id to support the campaign. Once he click support button, the campaign link/URL will be post in his FB wall and share the post with his friends via FB messenger.
